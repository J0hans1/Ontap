Tetsting Document

A brief summary of the questionnaire you send out to testers, as well as the link to the Google Form you used. Don't forget to ask your client to test the website. The questions we used could be put into three categories. First what kind of device they were testing from and what browser they used. This information is very important to us as we can see if we have concrete problems with a certain device or browser. The second category would be who they are and how they fit our target audience. If the user giving feedback is in our target audience their opinion might be more useful than someone who is not in our target audience. The last category would be the feedback itself. This is of course important to let us know what the user thought was good, bad and what could be changed. We used both live testing and testing via a Google Form.

Our experience of this project as a whole is that it can be extremely valueable to work on some bigger projects like this from time to time.
